# Frequently Asked Questions

## Q: Will my files be permanently deleted?
**A:** Safe Mode ON (default) = preview only. Safe Mode OFF = real deletion. Always test first!

## Q: Is it safe to delete Temp files?
**A:** Yes! Temp files are temporary and won't affect system. Safe to delete.

## Q: How often should I cleanup?
**A:** Weekly or monthly. Use Schedule feature for automation.

## Q: Can I recover deleted files?
**A:** Windows Recycle Bin keeps them 30 days. Or restore from backup.

## Q: Why is Cache so large?
**A:** Browsers cache everything - videos, images, scripts. Safe to delete.

## Q: Does cleanup slow my PC?
**A:** No! Frees space = faster. Removes unnecessary files.

## Q: Can I whitelist folders?
**A:** Yes! Add to whitelist in Settings: `Documents,Photos,Important`

## Q: Does AI need internet?
**A:** Gemini API = yes. If offline, cleanup works in Safe mode.

## Q: What if cleanup fails?
**A:** Check logs tab. File may be in use. Retry later.

## Q: PowerShell execution policy error?
**A:** Run: `Set-ExecutionPolicy -ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned -Scope CurrentUser`
